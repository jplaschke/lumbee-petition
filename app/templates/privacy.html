Lumbee Tribe of North Carolina

🔒 Privacy & Data Use Policy

Your personal data submitted through this petition is used only to verify your eligibility to sign and to support certification of this petition by the Tribal Council. It will be encrypted and deleted after the petition results are certified.

What information we collect

When you sign this petition, we collect your full legal name, enrollment ID, email address, optional phone number, and an uploaded copy of your Lumbee membership ID. We also record the IP address the submission came from and the date and time you signed.

How we use this information

This information is used exclusively for two purposes:

  • Verifying eligibility — confirming that each signer is a registered enrolled member of the Lumbee Tribe, and preventing duplicate signatures from the same enrollment ID.
  • Certification by the Tribal Council — providing the Petition Committee and Tribal Council the records needed to certify that this petition meets the requirements of Article V of the Lumbee Constitution.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party for marketing or any other unrelated purpose. We do not use your data for any purpose beyond what's described on this page.

How we protect your information

Sensitive personal fields (your name, email, and phone number) are encrypted at rest in our database. Each recorded signature is also digitally signed by our system at the moment it's submitted, creating a tamper-evident record that proves your signature entry has not been altered after the fact.

Data retention and deletion

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to complete the petition and certification process. Once the Tribal Council has certified the results of this petition, your personal identifying information will be deleted from our systems. Aggregate, de-identified signature counts may be retained for historical record-keeping.

Your uploaded ID

The membership ID image you upload is used solely to verify your enrollment status. It is stored separately from publicly-accessible areas of our website and is not displayed publicly at any point.

Questions

If you have questions about how your information is used or want to request early deletion of your data, please contact the Petition Committee through the contact information provided on the ordinance page.
